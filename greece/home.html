A Chronicle of My Greece Trip

May 13 - 22, 2022

In the summer of 2022, my family and I went on a vacation to the beautiful Mediterranean country of Greece. Over 10 days, we visited countless attractions, from ancient relics like the Acropolis to luxury spots in Santorini to beautiful ocean waters off the coast of Zakynthos. There were so many amazing experiences during our time there, and it was truly a vacation to remember. As for this travel log, I brought along my Smiski collectibles and thought it would be fun to document our experiences from their "eyes", written in letter format to my girlfriend Kathy. Enjoy!
